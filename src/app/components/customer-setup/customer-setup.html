How It Works

The setup flow is the same for all customers — but what happens after depends on the management model you choose.

You always configure first

Regardless of model, you create the customer profile, assign locations, allocate connector pools, and define connections. This setup wizard walks you through each step.

Then, depending on the management model…

Provider Managed

You stay in control
2

You deploy and manage all connectors and configurations on behalf of the customer.

3

The customer can view their devices and activate them using codes you provide — no IT knowledge required on their end.

4

Ongoing changes, troubleshooting, and access management run through you.

Best for customers without dedicated IT staff.

Customer Self-Managed

They take the wheel
2

The customer's admin is invited to their portal to review the resources you've allocated.

3

Their admin installs connectors, configures hosted apps, and manages day-to-day access for their sites.

4

You remain the resource owner and can step in or reallocate at any time, but routine management stays with them.

Best for customers with an IT manager or site admin.
